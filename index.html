about our

tea station

Born in 1999 in Korle Bu, Accra, Tea Station is the brainchild of founder Francis Patawah. Emigrating from Ghana, Francis embraced a journey of diverse professions until he discovered his true passion in 2019 after working at Fresh mix located at Union Tpke Queens, NY. Realizing the American preference for colder beverages, he innovatively merged traditional tea with popular local tastes. Thus, Tea Station was born, introducing a blend of iced teas and boba drinks, while still preserving the essence of traditional Taiwanese and Chinese teas. Dive into our menu and embark on a delightful tea and call for custom orders experience! Join us on this refreshing journey, as we celebrate not just tea, but the spirit of innovation and cultural fusion that defines us. Cheers!

Aged Tea

Gradual oxidation is one of the main changes that occur as tea leaves age. Oxidation is a natural chemical process that begins as soon as the tea leaves are plucked from the bush. Processing fresh teas often involves stopping, accelerating, or halting the oxidation process midway at the desired point.
